Meet Our Mt Pleasant Dental Team

Creating Countless Smiles

The average team member has been with our practice for over a decade, and that’s because they love what they do and who they serve. Their experience and dedication will be felt whether you talk to them on the phone, via email, or in person, as they will do everything they can to make your time with us as pleasant and productive as possible.

Angela L., Front Office Coordinator

Angela has been a part of the dental world for over a decade, all of which has been spent here at Smileworks! She is a Clemson graduate and strives to create a magical experience for each of our patients. Outside of work, she likes to travel, go boating, visit the beach, and make memories with family and friends.

Beige, Front Office Coordinator

For more than a decade, Beige has dedicated herself to delivering the best customer service possible. She is a Mount Pleasant native and attended the College of Charleston. Married with two children, she likes to spend her free time boating, traveling, and being with family.

Brooke, Front Office Coordinator

Brooke says that the best thing about her job is the ability to make a tangible impact by directly helping people through her work, which gives her a sense of fulfillment and motivates her to continue learning and growing in her role. A Charleston native, she says that her dog, Petunia, is her world, so most of her free time is dedicated to spoiling her!

Starr, Dental Assistant/Front Office Coordinator

Starr says that she’s here to make sure everyone who enters our practice feels like family and leaves with a big smile! She completed her dental training through Trident Technical College and has been with Smileworks for her entire career.

When she’s not in the office, you’ll usually find Starr watching Clemson football, hanging out on the water, trying a new coffee spot, spending time with family, and loving on all the fur babies in her life.

Kara, Registered Dental Hygienist

Kara has been with us for over a decade and a part of dentistry for 25+ years. Her dental hygiene training was done through Onondaga College, and her big goal every day is to make sure each of her patients has a comfortable experience.

Coming to us from Hamilton, NY, Kara loves being outdoors, so you’ll often find her boating or kayaking if she isn’t traveling or trying a new restaurant.

Storm, Registered Dental Hygienist

Nothing makes Storm happier than seeing someone become more confident because our team helped them achieve a healthier smile. A graduate of Florence Darlington Technical College, she was an Expanded Duties Dental Assistant before becoming a hygienist, giving her plenty of experience with all aspects of working in a dental office.

Storm grew up in a large family with lots of brothers and sisters, and she’s so happy that her boyfriend has seamlessly become part of their tight-knit group. When she isn’t having a great time with loved ones, she likes to travel, read, and be anywhere that’s hot and sunny.

Michelle, Registered Dental Hygienist

Michelle is a 35+ year dental veteran, and we’re thrilled to have someone with her level of experience on our team. She started her dental journey as an assistant in high school and eventually graduated summa cum laude from the hygiene program at St. Petersburg College. 

Michelle married her high school prom date, who is an aerospace engineer. His work saw them move to Florida where they had two beautiful daughters. Gail, Registered Dental Hygienist

Gail has spent an amazing 35+ years in dentistry, and she says she still loves building connections with our patients as well as her coworkers while doing a job that serves the community. She was trained at the University of Michigan and has worked in private practice for her entire career, except for a year and a half on an army base in Italy!

When she’s not at the office, Gail likes to exercise, read, cook, and be with family and friends.

Angela H., Registered Dental Hygienist

Angela says it’s the people she works with that make her job truly special, referring to both our patients as well as her fellow team members! She earned her B.S. in Dental Hygiene through West Virginia University and is originally from Kittanning, PA.

Olivia, Registered Dental Hygienist

Tracy, Dental Assistant

Maddie, Registered Dental Hygienist
