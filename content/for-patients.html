How to Get Started with Us

We love welcoming new patients through our doors and helping them feel right at home, but you probably have a few questions first! Below, you’ll find information about what will happen during your initial visit as well as our payment options. If there is anything else you’d like to know, you can always reach out to our team.

Your First Visit

You can expect your first cleaning and exam appointment to take about two hours. We understand this is longer compared to other offices, but this is because we want to truly get to know you as a person while fully understanding your oral health. This also gives us plenty of time to capture images, go over them with you, and discuss future treatment options in detail so you walk out of our office with complete certainty.

Dental Insurance

Our practice accepts Delta Dental Premier as well as many other popular dental insurance plans. We can answer any questions you might have about your coverage, provide an estimate of your out-of-pocket costs, and we’ll even handle the claim for you. Give us a call today if you’d like to know more about how we could use your coverage.

CareCredit Financing

With CareCredit, a lack of insurance or inability to pay for everything right now isn’t a problem. They make it easy to get the treatments you need today and break up the cost into smaller installments in line with your budget.
