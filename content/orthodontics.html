How Invisalign Works

All the patient has to do is wear a progressive series of clear aligners that fit over the teeth. They will be designed based on a highly detailed scan of the mouth, and each one is uniquely shaped to gently push the teeth into the correct positions. After wearing one set for 20-22 hours a day for a week or two, a patient simply moves on to the next set.

The Benefits of Invisalign Clear Aligners

  • Subtle: Everyone can tell when someone has braces, but Invisalign practically disappears when worn.
  • Comfort: The smooth plastic of the trays is much gentler on the gums, lips, and cheeks compared to regular braces.
  • Convenient: You can take the trays off to eat or brush your teeth, meaning maintaining your regular diet and oral hygiene routine is easy.
