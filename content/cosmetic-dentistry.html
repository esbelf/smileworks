Veneers

These thin, porcelain shells sit on the fronts of teeth and hide a wide range of smile flaws. Depending on your case, they can be used to treat stains, chips, cracks, and even gaps in as little as two appointments.

Metal-Free Restorations

Our cosmetic dentists use the latest advancements in dentistry, and our filling material is a composite resin that contains no metals, bonds well to teeth, and adds strength to the remaining tooth structure. Our crowns are also made of natural-looking materials, and their shade can be matched to the color of the nearby teeth while masking a wide variety of flaws

Dental Bonding

Small gaps and even chips can detract from your overall smile. By using tooth-colored resin, we can lengthen or repair teeth. Let us use the artistic form of cosmetic bonding to erase those imperfections so you can smile with confidence again.

Teeth Whitening

Our teeth whitening treatments use special bleaching gel to dramatically brighten your enamel. Whether it’s done in-office or with a take-home kit will depend on your needs. In the latter case, our custom trays would be enough for you to see great results in just a few days!

Smile Makeovers

When your grin has more than one kind of flaw, a smile makeover could be in order. This option fuses several cosmetic services into one big treatment, meaning it can do quite a lot. A given makeover may involve teeth whitening, veneers, cosmetic bonding, metal-free restorations, and more. The exact services included will depend on your needs, so you can trust it’ll do your grin a great deal of good.

Virtual Smile Design

Committing to cosmetic dentistry can be hard if you don’t know how it’ll look. Smileworks solves that problem with virtual smile design, which can show you a given procedure’s results. All we’d have to do is take a photo of your mouth and alter it to simulate teeth whitening, veneer placement, and so on. You can even take the final image home to show friends and family.
